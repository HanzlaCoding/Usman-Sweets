Our Story

A Family Legacy,
Sweeter Every Year

What began as a humble mithai stall in Township's Main Market has grown into one of Lahore's most beloved sweet institutions. For over four decades, the family behind Usman Sweets has risen before dawn, hand-crafted fresh mithai, and served the neighbourhood with love and pride.

Known across Lahore as the home of the softest, juiciest Gulab Jamun in the city — our recipe hasn't changed since day one. Customers travel from across Punjab just for a kilo. That's the Usman promise.
